The Atlas of Canada - Website Redesign

The old

The current home page of the Atlas of Canada does not provide users with the best experience. The Federal Identity Program and the Canada Style Guide both ensure an authoritative, professional, and trusted look and feel across all Canada.ca websites. That being said, there was room for improvement in terms of user-experience based on what people are looking for.

After conducting the necessary research, reorganizing the information architecture of the site became possible. Next, considering design improvements to guide users through their journey intuitively was achieved through icons and imagery. I designed a banner which displays the Atlas of Canada's graphic identifier, as well as two map products combined to represent the innovation and historical significance across the Atlas.
