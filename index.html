About Me

Hi there! I'm Jenny, a Project Officer and Multimedia Designer at Natural Resources Canada in Ottawa, ON. I first joined the team during a co-op while studying at Algonquin College, and started my full-time role after graduating from the Interactive Media Design program in 2023.
While my main focus is graphic design, I also have experience across various design and communications fields. This page highlights my skills and some of my favourite design projects. Feel free to reach out, I'd love to connect!
