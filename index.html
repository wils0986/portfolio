About Me

Hi, I'm Jenny! I graduated with honours from Algonquin College's Interactive Media Design program in December 2023. During my co-op placement at Natural Resources Canada, I worked as an in-house graphic designer, which led to subsequent contract work supporting the department's design and communications projects. I'm based in Ottawa, Ontario, and I'm always excited to explore new opportunities.



I'm passionate about graphic and UX design and I love developing web experiences that are visually engaging, accessible, and intuitive. I'm driven by problem-solving and I believe in design that strikes a balance between user needs and business goals. I'm also a cat lover, and they make occasional appearances in my work.
