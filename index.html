About Me

I'm a recent Interactive Media Design graduate from Algonquin College, based in Ontario, Canada. I completed my co-op placement at Natural Resources Canada as an in-house graphic designer, which led into contract work.

I'm passionate about graphic and UX design and love developing web experiences that are visually pleasing, accessible, and intuitive. I'm driven by problem-solving and enjoy designing solutions that support both user needs with business objectives. I'm always excited to learn, grow, and contribute to thoughtful design solutions. I'm also a cat lover, and they make occasional appearances in my work.
